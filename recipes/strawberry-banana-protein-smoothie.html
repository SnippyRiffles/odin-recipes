Strawberry Banana Protein Smoothie

Description

This strawberry-banana protein smoothie with almonds and protein powder is great for a meal replacement or after a workout.

Ingredients

  • 1 Â¼ cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 10 whole almonds
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup ice cubes
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate flavored protein powder

Steps

  1. Place strawberries, banana, almonds, and water into a blender
  2. Blend to combine
  3. Add ice cubes and puree until smooth
  4. Add protein powder and mix until evenly incorporated, about 30 seconds.
