Braised Chicken Thighs

Description

Attracted to the simplicity of meal prepping, but not the actual results? This braised chicken thigh recipe is easy to make without all of the rigidness that comes with traditional meal prepping. If you have 20 minutes to spare you can easily make 10 pounds of this recipe. The best part? While the chicken is cooking you are free to do whatever you typically do in an evening at home.

Ingredients

  • chicken thighs (boneless, skinless)
  • hot water (or chicken stock for more flavor)
  • onion
  • rosemary
  • salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Peel onion and cut into 4 wedges
  3. Remove chicken from packaging and place in an oven safe dish
  4. Add onion, salt, and rosemary to dish with chicken
  5. Pour hot water (or chicken stock) over chicken
  6. Place in oven for 2-3 hours
